# Nutrition Protocol

## History
- Started 2026 at 72-74kg, ~20-22% BF, strongest in his life
- Cut ~1kg/week for 3 months (aggressive)
- Stalled in month 4, adherence dropped
- Currently 62.8kg at 166cm, estimated 15-16% BF
- Lost ~3kg lean tissue during cut (illness + training stoppage)
- Lean mass at peak: ~56.2kg. Current estimated lean mass: ~53kg.

## Current Protocol (established May 29, 2026)
- **Phase:** Maintenance / slight surplus (STOP CUTTING)
- **Calories:** ~2200-2400 kcal/day
- **Protein:** 130-140g/day minimum (2g/kg+)
- **Carbs:** High, prioritized around training (rice, oats, potatoes, fruit)
- **Fats:** Moderate, don't restrict
- **Water:** 3L+ daily (fullness, vascularity, face health)
- **Reduce:** Alcohol, excess sodium (face bloat)

## Rationale
- At 62.8kg/166cm he's lean enough — needs muscle fullness, not more fat loss
- Glycogen refill provides immediate visual improvement (1-2cm on arms within days)
- Further cutting = flatter, smaller, worse looking in a t-shirt
- Retatrutide NOT warranted at his weight — would accelerate muscle loss
- 15-16% BF is the "worst looking zone" — too lean to look big, too high to look shredded
- With food + training, muscle memory will restore volume rapidly

## Peak Week Protocol (final 3-4 days before target date)
1. Increase water to 4-5L for 3 days
2. Drop to 1.5L the day before
3. Reduce sodium for 2 days
4. Salty carb-heavy meal the night before
5. Train arms morning of or day before (pump lingers)
