- **Rationale:** Won't show abs to friends. Arms and face are what matter. This means fullness > leanness.

- **Rationale:** Myonuclei persist. Previously trained muscle regains size in fraction of original build time. Glycogen refill is immediate (days).

### RETATRUTIDE REJECTED
- **Decision:** Do not use retatrutide
- **Rationale:** Not warranted at 62.8kg. Designed for obesity. Would accelerate muscle loss — opposite of goal.

- **Rationale:** Heavy caffeine user (vasoconstriction), wants vascularity, has forearm blood flow issues from gaming. Offsets caffeine downsides while keeping cognitive benefits. Low risk profile. Available legally in Ireland via online prescription.

### BPC-157 CONDITIONAL
- **Decision:** Use only if forearm strain is genuinely preventing consistent training
- **Rationale:** The value isn't the peptide itself — it's enabling 4x/week training. If he can train without it, save the money.

- **Decision:** Last caffeine by 4-5pm
- **Rationale:** Half-life of 5-6hrs means evening caffeine degrades deep sleep even if he falls asleep fine. Protects recovery.

### MODAFINIL REJECTED
- **Decision:** Do not use modafinil for 5am commute days
- **Rationale:** Family history of brain blood clot (grandfather, age 58). Modafinil increases clotting risk. Gaming lifestyle = sedentary. Risk > benefit.

- **Rationale:** Eliminates 3hr sleep disaster entirely. Travel evening before, maintain normal sleep window, normal recovery.

- **Rationale:** Creatine non-negotiable for muscle fullness. Citrulline for pump/blood flow (gaming strain). Beta-alanine for training capacity.
